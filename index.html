My Story

Intro

Born and raised in Nigeria, West Africa. Growing up in Nigeria was memorable, the fighting spirit was something I had to make part and parcel of me. The hustle and bustle in Nigeria is a norm. The most challenging part was finding a way to stay out of trouble, be creative and industrous while living in an environment with lots of vices around, average daily electricity supply of about 0-3 hours, horrendous roads and the likes.
When I couldn't code because of the obvious power issue, I spent my time raising catfishes, poultry and planting vegetables etc.

Journey Into Technology

I wasn't so privileged to use Computers growing up, even though my Dad had a huge computer (Windows 95) on his reading desk with lots of floppy disks around, I never knew how to use a computer, partly because we had no electricity (which is so scare in Nigeria) and whenever we did have electricity, my Dad would be using it do his work.
Toward the end of my High School education we were taught BASIC programming language, I found it so interesting, I noticed I enjoyed the flowcharts too and the flow of logic, programming was so interesting then. We wrote code on a piece of paper for quizzes and exams in high school then because we had no computers to run the code.
I chose to study Computer Science in University, even though up till that moment (August 2010) I never had a computer, I had no email account, I couldn't use a computer, but I was fascinated about having a flowchart or logic on paper and making it a reality with few lines of code.
Few days before going to the University I got a Laptop. During the first week of lectures I wrote my first program using MY OWN Laptop and I have never stopped coding since then.
I learnt a lot in the School and did lots of projects too. Hangman Game (Visual Basic), Computer Assisted Instructor (C), Prototype Auction System (PHP, MySQL) and lots more.

Hobbies and Interests

I love coding and thinking of logic, there's nothing that makes me happier; I love gardening; I watch and listen to news everyday; I enjoy engaging in meaningful discussions about culture, politics, religion name it; Lastly I love learning, I can learn from a one-year old as long as I find something worth learning and emulating.
I am also a very good teacher.

Todo

Project Description

Organises your Todo tasks into groups, you can customise task groups to have unique colour themes and also adjust the priority (Normal or high).
Some of its features are listed below:

  • A single-page application that makes use of modals and AJAX requests on every CRUD operation without refreshing the page.
  • There's the option to set due dates to task items. A "DUE" icon appears on the top right of tasks that are due.
  • Search field to search based on task items or groups.
  • Sorts and arranges the groups and tasks alphabetically on load and when a new group/task is added.
  • You can have a look at the summary of your task groups (A tree diagram of all your groups and tasks; A bar chart showing you what groups have what color themes) using SVG and d3.js Library.
  • High Priority groups have a red exclamation icon on the top-right corner to let the user identify it.
  • Clean and easy to use with legible fonts and adequate white spacing.

Project Languages

  • Javascript
  • MEAN (MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS, NodeJS)
