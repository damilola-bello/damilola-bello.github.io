My Story

Intro

Born and raised in Nigeria, West Africa.

Journey Into Technology

Toward the end of my High School education we were taught BASIC programming language, I found it so interesting, I noticed I enjoyed the flowcharts too and the flow of logic, programming was so interesting then. We wrote code on a piece of paper for quizzes and exams in high school then because we had no computers to run the code.
I chose to study Computer Science at University, even though up till that moment (August 2010) I never had a computer, I had no email account, I couldn't use a computer, but I was fascinated about having a flowchart or logic on paper and making it a reality with few lines of code.
Few days before going to the University I got a Laptop. During the first week of lectures, I wrote my first program and I have never stopped coding since then.

Todo

Project Description

Organises your Todo tasks into groups, you can customise task groups to have unique colour themes and also adjust the priority (Normal or high).
Some of its features are listed below:

  • A single-page application that makes use of modals and AJAX requests on every CRUD operation without refreshing the page.
  • There's the option to set due dates to task items. A "DUE" icon appears on the top right of tasks that are due.
  • Search field to search based on task items or groups.
  • Sorts and arranges the groups and tasks alphabetically on load and when a new group/task is added.
  • You can have a look at the summary of your task groups (A tree diagram of all your groups and tasks; A bar chart showing you what groups have what color themes) using SVG and d3.js Library.
  • High Priority groups have a red exclamation icon on the top-right corner to let the user identify it.
  • Clean and easy to use with legible fonts and adequate white spacing.

Project Languages

  • Javascript
  • MEAN (MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS, NodeJS)
